# sample_40_en

Small committed calibration fixture for the general agent-memory reliability
benchmark (40 English scenarios). Intended for quick scorer/baseline sanity
checks and CI, not as a full evaluation set.

- `scenarios.jsonl` — 40 scenarios (`retrace_bench_general_1` schema).
- `manifest.json` — dataset metadata.

Larger datasets (`dev_800_en`, `stress_1760_en`) are generated artifacts and are
intentionally not committed.
